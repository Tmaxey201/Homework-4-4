About Me

Thomas Maxey is the current administrator of a chain of privately ran pre-schools in Eastern, North Carolina. With a background in writing and a degree in English from the University of North Carolina- Wilmington, the daily necessity of keeping all those involved working together in an efficient and cohesive manner is managed with an emphasis on communication. Thomas is fueled by his innate drive to contribute to something bigger than himself and a passion for expressing himself in a plethora of ways. His neverending perseverance has been the backbone in reaching his goals throughout his life. From being a division one collegiate shotputter to contributing toward the success of a small business in his hometown, there is no end to what his perseverance can achieve. With new times come new goals, and right now he has set his sights on pursuing a new career in the tech field. He is currently enrolled in a full-stack web development boot camp with the goal of getting a job as soon as possible.
